Voyeur Roma

Fineas Jackson

May 27, 2023 • 2 min read

Surrounded by countless wonders of the world, Lucia works. Now, she is positioned in front of the Vatican obelisk, crouched over a nail. With two small metal rods she tries to dig it out. People gather to watch her; some shake their heads and mutter under their breath. They think she is crazy. What they don’t understand, is that this is all by design. Lucia is an undercover operative working at the most discreet organization in Italy, Unità Segreta Operativa, or USO.

Lucia goes back to picking at the nail wedged into the cobblestones. People come to the Vatican and try to steal the stones; Lucia’s job is to stop them before they get a chance. This nail is wedged in deep though, so Lucia radios for help. A few minutes later, her teammate Lorenzo comes with a pair of industrial steel wire cutters and pulls out the nail.

Lucia sighs from exhaustion and heads over to the small fountain nearby. She runs the cool water through her dark brown hair. Then she dunks her head into the fountain pool. A tourist gasps nearby, as Lucia pulls her dripping head from the fountain. At this, Lucia chuckles inwardly. The crazier they think she is, the better. No one suspects a crazy lady, so she hears all sorts of things.

Plots to steal valuable artifacts from the basilica. Plans to smuggle in illicit substances under the pretense of a religious pilgrimage. Hushed discussions plotting against the Vatican's political interests. All information makes its way to her, unnoticed. People shift away from her as she walks by, as if Lucia carries a disease. But they don’t stop talking. And this is what makes Lucia so good; it’s why she is recognized as the best at USO.

Back in the square, Lorenzo is now fumbling with the nail, examining it with a careful eye. Lucia approaches him, leaving a trail of water droplets behind. "Find anything interesting?" she asks casually. Lorenzo grunts in response, not looking up.

"Seems to be a GPS tracker embedded in this," he murmurs, prying at the nail with a small, delicate tool.

"Again?" Lucia feigns surprise, leaning in to get a closer look at the seemingly innocuous object. Lorenzo nods, handing over the nail to Lucia who takes it with a firm grip. She inspects it closely, a hint of a smirk playing on her lips. Just another day at the Vatican.
